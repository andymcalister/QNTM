# QNTM Personal Trading Policy
Purpose: prevent scalping/front-running and preserve the "bona fide / disinterested publisher" posture under the Investment Advisers Act publisher's exclusion.

1. QNTM LLC holds no securities and executes no trades.
2. Covered persons: the founder and any principals or control persons of QNTM LLC.
3. No trading ahead of publication. Covered persons may not buy or sell a scored security in anticipation of a signal or signal change before it is published to subscribers.
4. Trade only on published signals. Any personal trade in a scored security must rely solely on information already visible to all subscribers.
5. Bright line = published_at. For any signal batch, no covered-person trade in an affected security is permitted before that batch's signals_published_at timestamp. The audit log is the record of compliance.
6. No reaction trading. No trade designed to benefit from subscriber response to a signal (e.g., buy, then publish HIGH, then sell into demand).
7. Recordkeeping. Log every personal trade in a scored security (date, ticker, action) and retain it.
8. Public disclosure of this conflict is maintained in the Investment Disclaimer.
